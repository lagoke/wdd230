Discover Atlanta

Atlanta, the capital city of the state of Georgia in the United States, has a rich history and many interesting features that make it a fascinating destination for travelers. One of the most notable things about Atlanta is its abundance of trees and green spaces, which has earned it the nickname "city in a forest". Despite being a major urban center, Atlanta is home to over 100 parks and nature preserves, including the expansive Piedmont Park in Midtown, which covers 189 acres and features walking trails, sports facilities, and a large lake. Overall, Atlanta has something for everyone, from nature lovers and history buffs to art enthusiasts and sports fans, making it a must-visit destination in the United States.

CNN Center

The CNN Center is a large commercial complex located in downtown Atlanta, Georgia, United States. It is the world headquarters of CNN and one of the most popular tourist destinations in Atlanta. The complex covers an area of over 2 million square feet and includes offices, studios, retail shops, restaurants, and attractions.The center offers a variety of guided tours that provide visitors with a behind-the-scenes look at the inner workings of CNN. Visitors can see the newsroom, control rooms, and production studios, and learn about the history of the network and its impact on the world of news.!

Centennial Olympic Park

Centennial Olympic Park is a public park located in downtown Atlanta, Georgia, United States. The park was created for the 1996 Summer Olympics and covers an area of 21 acres. It serves as a central gathering place for locals and visitors alike and offers a variety of attractions and events. The park features several notable landmarks, such as the Fountain of Rings, which is a computer-controlled water fountain that is synchronized with music and light shows. The park also includes a variety of sculptures, memorials, and public art installations that celebrate the history and culture of Atlanta and the Olympic Games. In addition to its landmarks, Centennial Olympic Park hosts a variety of events throughout the year, such as concerts, festivals, and sporting events. The park also offers a range of recreational activities, such as walking paths, playgrounds, and open spaces for picnics and games.

Georgia Acquarium

The Georgia Aquarium is a world-class aquarium located in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. It is one of the largest aquariums in the world and houses over 100,000 animals from more than 500 different species. The aquarium covers an area of 10 million gallons and includes a variety of exhibits and attractions.The aquarium is divided into several galleries, each with a different theme and collection of animals. One of the most popular exhibits is the Ocean Voyager, which features a 6.3 million-gallon tank that is home to whale sharks, manta rays, and other ocean species. The aquarium also has exhibits featuring beluga whales, sea otters, penguins, and more. In addition to the animal exhibits, the Georgia Aquarium offers a variety of interactive experiences and programs, such as behind-the-scenes tours, animal encounters, and sleepovers. Visitors can also watch live animal feedings and attend educational presentations and shows

Lenox Mall

Lenox Mall is a premier shopping destination located in the Buckhead district of Atlanta, Georgia, United States. The mall features over 250 stores, including luxury brands such as Louis Vuitton, Prada, and Gucci, as well as popular retailers such as Apple, H&M, and Zara. The mall covers an area of over 1.5 million square feet and includes multiple levels with a variety of dining and entertainment options. In addition to shopping, visitors can enjoy a meal at one of the many restaurants, cafes, and food courts, or catch a movie at the onsite AMC theater.Lenox Mall is also known for its special events and promotions throughout the year, such as seasonal sales, fashion shows, and live music performances. The mall is open seven days a week and offers a variety of parking options , including valet and self-parking

MLK Birthplace

The Martin Luther King Jr. Birthplace is a historic site located in the Sweet Auburn neighborhood of Atlanta, Georgia, United States. It is the birthplace of Martin Luther King Jr., one of the most prominent figures in the American Civil Rights Movement. The site covers a two-block area and includes several buildings, such as the King family home, Ebenezer Baptist Church, and the King Center for Nonviolent Social Change. The King family home is a two-story, Victorian-style house where Martin Luther King Jr. was born on January 15, 1929. The home has been restored to its original condition and is open to visitors for guided tours. The tours provide insight into the early life of Martin Luther King Jr. and his family, as well as the historical context of their experiences in the segregated South.Ebenezer Baptist Church is where Martin Luther King Jr. and his father were pastors.

Six Flags

Six Flags Over Georgia is a theme park located in Austell, Georgia, United States. The park covers an area of 290 acres and features over 40 rides and attractions, including roller coasters, water rides, and family-friendly rides. Six Flags Over Georgia is one of the largest theme parks in the Southeastern United States and attracts millions of visitors each year.Some of the park's most popular attractions include Goliath, a roller coaster that reaches speeds of up to 70 miles per hour and includes a 165-foot drop, and the Mind Bender, a classic steel coaster that features three inversions. The park also has a variety of other thrill rides, such as the Acrophobia, a free-fall ride that drops riders from a height of 200 feet. In addition to thrill rides, Six Flags Over Georgia has a variety of family-friendly attractions, including a children's area with rides designed for younger visitors . The park also hosts live entertainment shows, such as musical performances and stunt shows, throughout the day.

Zoo Atlanta

Zoo Atlanta is a zoological park located in the city of Atlanta, Georgia, United States. It was founded in 1889 and is one of the oldest continuously operating zoos in the country. The zoo covers an area of 40 acres and is home to more than 1,000 animals from over 200 species, including elephants, gorillas, pandas, lions, and tigers. One of the highlights of Zoo Atlanta is its giant panda exhibit, which features two adult pandas and their cubs. The zoo is also known for its conservation efforts, particularly in the areas of gorilla and elephant conservation. In addition to animal exhibits, Zoo Atlanta offers a variety of educational programs and interactive experiences for visitors of all ages, including behind-the-scenes tours, animal encounters, and a petting zoo. The zoo also has several dining options and gift shops.

Ponce City Market

Located at the nexus of Atlanta’s most established neighborhoods, Ponce City Market is the latest incarnation of a long legacy of community-centered built environments along this iconic stretch of Ponce de Leon Avenue. The National Trust for Historic Preservation recognizes Ponce City Market as “History in the Making,” and part of a plan “to move Atlanta forward while maintaining and emphasizing the city’s unique history and culture.” From ground-floor retail and restaurants, to the Flats’ amenity-rich residences, to creative office space, to an amusement park on the Roof, Ponce City Market is the culmination of its history
