Our Story

Bountiful Foods was founded in 2010 by a couple who shared a passion for fresh, delicious food and a commitment to sustainability. They saw a need for a local business that could provide the community with the freshest, highest-quality produce available, and decided to start their own. They began by selling their produce at local farmers markets and quickly gained a following. People loved the freshness and variety of their offerings, as well as their commitment to supporting local farms and reducing food waste. As the business grew, the founders decided to open a brick-and-mortar store in Carlsbad, California. They wanted to create a space where people could come together and enjoy fresh, healthy food and drink, while also learning about where their food comes from and how it's produced.


Our Mission

At Bountiful Foods, our mission is to provide our customers with the freshest, highest-quality fruit and produce available, along with a selection of specialty fruit drinks that are ready to serve. We believe in sourcing our ingredients from local, sustainable farms whenever possible, and we strive to offer a diverse and ever-changing selection of seasonal produce. We are committed to delivering excellent customer service and to creating a welcoming, inclusive atmosphere for everyone.
